    Food reference

    Sunday 24 May · No nuts, seeds, or whole grains

    Avoid from today

    • All nuts (almonds, peanuts, cashews, etc.)
    • All seeds (chia, flax, sesame, sunflower, poppy)
    • Wholemeal / multigrain bread
    • Brown rice, oats, porridge, muesli, granola
    • Weet-Bix, bran cereals
    • Quinoa, couscous, popcorn

    Otherwise eat normally

    • White bread, white rice, white pasta
    • Meat, fish, eggs, dairy
    • Most fruit & veg (no nuts/seeds)
    • Plenty of water
    Monday 25 May · The White Diet

    Permitted

    • Milk, plain yoghurt, butter, margarine
    • Vanilla ice cream, white chocolate
    • Plain cream cheese, cottage, ricotta, fetta, sour cream
    • Egg whites, mayo, cream, white sauce
    • White rice, white pasta, peeled potatoes
    • Rice Bubbles, plain white bread/toast (not high-fibre), plain rice crackers
    • Chicken breast (no skin), grilled white fish

    Excluded white foods

    • Pears, parsnip, cauliflower, onion
    • High-fibre white breads (Wonder White)
    • Tofu, coconut
    • Porridge, banana, mushrooms
    • Semolina, couscous, popcorn

    Sample menu

    • Breakfast: Rice Bubbles with milk, OR white toast and scrambled egg (whites only)
    • Morning tea: Plain rice crackers with fetta
    • Lunch: Sliced chicken breast, butter, white bread sandwich
    • Afternoon tea: Plain vanilla yoghurt or vanilla milkshake
    • Early dinner: Plain grilled white fish + white sauce, white rice/pasta/peeled potato, vanilla ice cream or lemonade icy-pole
    Tuesday 26 May · Clear fluids only + Picoprep

    Approved clear fluids

    • Water (lots of it)
    • Clear broth, bonox, consommé, stock
    • Apple or pear juice — no pulp
    • Black tea or black coffee (no milk)
    • Clear sports drinks (Gatorade, Powerade, Hydralyte)
    • Lemonade, soft drinks, clear cordials
    • Plain jelly (not red/blue/purple)
    • Barley sugar
    • Moderate alcohol is allowed

    Not allowed

    • Any solid food
    • Anything red, blue, or purple in colour
    • Juice with pulp
    • Milk, milky tea/coffee, smoothies
    • Cream, yoghurt, ice cream

    Clear means you can see your fingers through the glass.

    Wednesday 27 May · Procedure day

    Nil by mouth as instructed by the hospital — nothing to eat, drink, suck or chew.

    One exception: the final Picoprep sachet (around 5:00 am) and water to take it with.

    Arrive at the admissions area at the time the hospital gave you, allowing time to complete admission.

    Medication notes

    • Iron tablets: stop one week before the procedure
    • Aspirin / Warfarin / Iscover / Plavix / Pradaxa or other blood thinners: continue as usual unless the gastroenterologist has told you otherwise. Tell them in advance if you're on any of these.
    • Other regular medications: take as usual with a sip of water on Wed morning.
